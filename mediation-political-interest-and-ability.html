Structural Equation Modelling of Political Trust and Ability

5 Mediation: political interest and ability

Having developed a reliable measurement model for our constructs, we are now prepared to specify the structural part of our model. Specifically, we consider a mediation model to explore the influence of political interest on political ability. We include the indirect effect of political interest through government trust. We also consider the influence of trust in people on government trust.

what is the effect of political interest and trust in government on how people conceive of their ability to participate in politics? Furthermore, is there a relationship between government trust and social trust?
